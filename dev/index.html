Search-term reference

The product picker accepts plain-language queries — synonyms are mapped automatically. Examples:

  • sleep ↔ insomnia
  • pain ↔ chronic pain, neuropathic, arthritis, fibromyalgia
  • anxiety ↔ GAD, panic
  • nausea ↔ chemo, CINV
  • BCP ↔ beta-caryophyllene, caryophyllene
  • MS ↔ multiple sclerosis
